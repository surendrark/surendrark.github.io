My work

Two-Echelon Vehicle Routing

Proposed mathematical models and heuristic algorithm for two-echelon vehicle roouting problems, which involves solving vehicle routing problems in two levels sequentially

GVRP on web

Developed a web interface where a user can create a network by clicking on the map and get the optimal routes as an output displayed on the open street map.

Electric Vehicle Routing

Proposed mathematical models and heuristic algorithm for electric vehicle routing problems with non-liner charging and load-dependent discharging

Demand Modelling

Heat map of freight trip generating establishments in Chennai city, India

Optimal Routes

Evaluated the effect of heterogeneous fleet on the optimal routing decisions

Travel Time Maps

Travel time map for Chennai city based on the GPS data collected
